🌱 Our Vision / आमचे ध्येय

Jambhulsangh was created to bring transparency, direct market access, and cutting-edge logistics support to local Jamun (Black Plum) farmers. We verify, digitize, and list every member to eliminate middle-agents and secure premium prices for growers.

  • Direct Farmer-to-Consumer network
  • 100% Genuine Devnagari Records
  • Quality Checked Organic Harvests

🍇 Premium Jamun Varieties

From the rich soil of our verified farms, our growers cultivate high-quality varieties of Jamun (Konkan Bahadoli, Goma Priyanka, and local selections) that are loaded with natural antioxidants, low glycemic index benefits, and rich flavor profiles.

  • Strictly Handpicked Harvesting
  • Perfect grading and packing
  • Ideal for health and diabetic diets
